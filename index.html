Welcome

My name is Thomas. I am a student at GBS and this is my "final project". I am really intrested in creating a YouTube channel and posting the best clips of other Twitch streamers for the specific game of my choosing. Making the YouTube channel was easy and simple, but making the video was not. First I had to find a professional in my topic. A streamer by the name "DrDisRespect", a Twitch affiliate and a professional in my topic. Not only does he have a HUGE Twitch channel with millions of followers, but he also has a Youtube channel with his best of clips of his stream. The editing is professional and cool. Using his channel as an inspiration..I went to my next step.

Now I had to learn how to use Adobe Preimere Pro. This is a editing software that my school provided. I watched Surfaced Studio's YouTube video on Adobe Preimere Pro for absolute beginners. This helped me learn how to put clips together and add cool transitions to them such as zoom in and wipe. After this step, I made the video. The picture below shows what it looked like. After that was done I posted the video to YouTube, a platform for me to show my product. The LAUNCH Cycle

The LAUNCH Cycle is an acronym used to help organize the developement stages. The L stands for Look, Lesson, and Learn. The A stands for Ask tons of questions. The U stands for Understand the problem/process. The N stands for Navigate ideas. The C stands for Create a prototype. The H stands for Highlight an fix. And all together that makes LAUNCH, to an auidence. Consult the link below if you need a refresher.
